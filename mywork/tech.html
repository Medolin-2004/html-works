About Our College

ABC Engineering College is one of the leading engineering institutions in Tamil Nadu. The college provides quality education, modern laboratories, experienced faculty members, and excellent placement opportunities. Every year, the college conducts a National Level Technical Symposium to encourage innovation and creativity among students.
